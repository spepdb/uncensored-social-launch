Core launch features

Feeds

New, Following & Trending

A true New feed, a Following feed just for people you follow, and a Trending feed that surfaces real activity instead of ad-driven “recommended” posts.

Profiles

Clean, expressive profiles

Simple profile pages with posts, media, follower counts, and a built-in link-in-bio so creators and businesses can send people where they actually want.

Messaging

End-to-end encrypted DMs

Direct messages designed from the start to be encrypted. Your conversations are between you and the other person — not you and a data center.

Moderation

Short, human rules

No vague “community standards” that can mean anything. Rules are short, specific, and easy to understand.

FAQ

Is this just a concept, or is code already live?

There’s already a working prototype with profiles, posting, and a feed. This page is about taking it from “dev toy” to a real launch-ready platform and funding the infrastructure behind it.

How are my email and phone used?

Only to contact you about launch, major updates, and possibly early access. No newsletters, no list selling, and you can opt out anytime.

Will it stay free to use?

The goal is yes. Core features should stay free. Crypto donations are how Uncensored Social avoids being forced into aggressive ads and surveillance.

What kind of content actually gets removed?

The focus is on spam, bots, and clearly illegal content — not controversial takes. The rules will be short, public, and written in normal language.

When will native apps be ready?

After the web app is stable, fast, and secure. The roadmap targets 2026 for Android & iOS builds, funded by this page and early backers.
